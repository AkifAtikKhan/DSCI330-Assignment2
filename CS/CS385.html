Course Description

  • CS 385: An introduction to basic Database Management Systems (DBMS) concepts. Topics include DBMS models including both relational and unstructured, query language, the design and normalization of database, database recovery and concurrency control. Prerequisites: CS 250 - Algorithms and Problem-Solving II and ENG 111 - College Reading and Writing
